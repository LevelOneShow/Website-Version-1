About Us:

Welcome to the Level One Show! We are a group that livestreams games, gaming podcasts, and anything otherwise related to gaming. Over the winter, we are going to be doing TWO charity livestreams. You can go to the navigation link titled "Stream & Donate" for more information.

Hosts

HalPlays

My name is Hal. I'm the Producer, Coordinator, and general Brain-Trust of the Level One Show. I first came up with the idea for the show during a sick day in March of 2014. I was inspired by thejarren's "Tux Show", hosted in 2013. Instead of focusing on interviewing famous YouTubers, I wanted to go deeper in the Gaming community. Lately, I've been coming up with more ideas to make the Level One Show brand more diverse, like Charity Livestreams. If you have any ideas or questions, feel free to tweet at me!

Murgo

Hey guys! My name is Murgo and I absolutely love video games. I am 14 living in a mans body exploring the world and telling my tales. Today I am here as the co-host on The Level One Show. Most of my personality can be categorized as overconfident, but that's what gives me my positive look on life.
