About Us:

Welcome to the Level One Show! We are a group that livestreams games, gaming podcasts, and anything otherwise related to gaming. Over the winter, we are going to be doing TWO charity livestreams. You can go to the navigation link titled "Stream & Donate" for more information.

Hosts

HalPlays

My name is Hal. I'm the Producer, Coordinator, and general Brain-Trust of the Level One Show. I first came up with the idea for the show during a sick day in March of 2014. I was inspired by thejarren's "Tux Show", hosted in 2013. Instead of focusing on interviewing famous YouTubers, I wanted to go deeper in the Gaming community. Lately, I've been coming up with more ideas to make the Level One Show brand more diverse, like Charity Livestreams. If you have any ideas or questions, feel free to tweet at me!

KuangSteak

Yo, what is up guys, my name is Nick, but you might know me better as KuangSteak. When Hal approached me with the idea of the Level One Show, I was super into the concept and I gladly agreed to help him start the show. I provided the original art for the stream and I co-commentated with Hal. When Hal approached me about expanding the brand, I decided I could help in more ways than just commentating. I brought up the idea of a website, and the rest of the crew perceived it very well. So, I set out to create it and it is what you see here now. However, I recently decided to take a more "backstage" role on the show and I will no longer be a host of the show after winter season one. If you have any questions, feel free to hit me up on twitter.
